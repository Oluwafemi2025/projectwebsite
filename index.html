Welcome to AFRICAN RENNAISANCE University

The university was founded with a clear mission to transform and elevate the standard of functional education within this region. As part of its unique structure, the institution adopts a collegiate system, which allows for more focused academic and administrative operations. Currently, it comprises five well-established colleges, each dedicated to nurturing excellence in their respective fields of study.

Our Vision

We strive to be a profound institution that raises competent and greatly intellectual protiges who will lead Africa to greatness

Our Mission

To ultimately establish brilliant minds and future leaders who will bring Africa and humanity as a whole to the greatest heights unimaginable to many

Glance Through ARU

For more than sixty years, ARU has delivered high-quality, research-driven education to Africans and others who have come through its doors in pursuit of knowledge.
